4 Portals are placed around the map and your objective is to destroy them to collect the power orbs. Depending on the portals, their health will vary. Once the portal is destroyed, the undead will spawn around you.

Power orbs are dropped from destroyed portals. Collecting all 4 power orbs will allow you to enter the blue portal and escape.

The undead will spawn in big groups around you once a portal destroys. They are slow but very hard.
